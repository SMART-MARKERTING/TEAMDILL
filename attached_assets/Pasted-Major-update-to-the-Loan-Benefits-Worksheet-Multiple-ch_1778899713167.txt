PART 7: NEW LOAN — AUTO-CALCULATE APR FROM CLOSING COSTS
============================================

In the "New Loan" input section:

REMOVE:
- Manual "New Loan APR" input field

ADD:
- "Total Closing Costs" input
  - Label: "Total Closing Costs"
  - Type: number, step 100, placeholder "5500"
  - Required field
  - Help text below: "Enter total estimated closing costs 
    (origination, title, recording, prepaids, etc.)"

CALCULATION:
The APR is now AUTO-CALCULATED based on:
- Loan amount
- Interest rate
- Term (years)
- Total closing costs (treated as prepaid finance charges)

Formula: Find the APR (annual rate) that, when applied to the 
net loan proceeds (loan amount minus closing costs), produces 
the same monthly payment as the stated rate on the full loan 
amount over the term.

Pseudocode:
- monthlyPayment = PMT(rate/12, term*12, loanAmount)
- netProceeds = loanAmount - closingCosts
- APR = solve for rate where PMT(apr/12, term*12, netProceeds) 
  equals monthlyPayment
- Use iterative bisection or Newton-Raphson to solve

Display the auto-calculated APR next to the rate in all 
snapshot and comparison tables:
"New Loan: $380,000 @ 6.500% Rate / 6.752% APR"

The APR field shown on the worksheet output should be 
READ-ONLY display, computed from the closing costs input.

PART 8: EXTRA MONTHLY PRINCIPAL — AUTO-CALCULATED AND LOCKED
============================================

The "Extra Monthly Contribution / Principal" field is now 
LOCKED (read-only, auto-calculated from the savings created).

CALCULATION LOGIC:

For Cash-Out Refinance and Rate Reduction:
- currentTotalOutflow = existingMortgageP&I + existingEscrow + 
  sum(debt min payments)
- newLoanPmt = monthly payment on new loan at stated rate
- newTotalOutflow = newLoanPmt + existingEscrow (if escrow 
  carries over, which it usually does on a refi)
- monthlySavings = currentTotalOutflow - newTotalOutflow
- extraMonthlyPrincipal = monthlySavings (auto-filled into 
  the calc engine)

For Home Equity Loan / HELOC:
- currentTotalOutflow = existingMortgageP&I + existingEscrow + 
  sum(debt min payments)
- newSecondLoanPmt = monthly payment on the new HELOAN/HELOC
- newTotalOutflow = existingMortgageP&I + existingEscrow + 
  newSecondLoanPmt
- monthlySavings = currentTotalOutflow - newTotalOutflow
- extraMonthlyPrincipal = monthlySavings (applied to the 
  SECOND mortgage / HELOC, not the first)

For Rate Reduction (no debt consolidation):
- currentTotalOutflow = existingMortgageP&I (no debts factored)
- newTotalOutflow = newLoanPmt
- monthlySavings = currentTotalOutflow - newTotalOutflow
- extraMonthlyPrincipal = monthlySavings

DISPLAY:
- The "Extra Monthly Principal" value still appears prominently 
  on the worksheet output
- Label changes to: "Auto-Applied Monthly Savings"
- Display copy: "$XXX/month — your calculated monthly savings, 
  automatically redirected to principal"
- This number cannot be edited by the user (locked field, 
  grayed out, or removed from the input panel entirely since 
  it's now derived)

If monthly savings is NEGATIVE (new payment is higher than 
current), display a warning instead:
"Note: With these inputs, the new loan does not produce 
monthly savings. This program may not be a fit — please 
adjust the inputs or consult Mykoal directly."
